CAD Lesson 3 – Extrude & Revolve

Block 1 – What Extrude Does

Turning 2D sketches into 3D solids; blind, symmetric, and up-to-face extrudes; add vs. remove vs. intersect; common sketch and direction mistakes.

Block 2 – Revolve

Revolving profiles around an axis; full vs. partial revolve; when revolve is better than extrude; common revolve errors like missing axis selection.

Block 3 – Competition Strategy: Extrude vs. Revolve

Recognizing revolve-friendly geometry from drawings; using thin extrudes and draft; choosing the fastest feature for SciOly-style parts.
